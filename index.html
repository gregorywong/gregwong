Oxfam Trailwalker Project

As an executive member of the Young Executives Club of the Hong Kong General Chamber of Commerce, I kickstarted its participation in Oxfam Trailwalker, a yearly fundraising event which requires teams of 4 hikers to complete the Maclehose Trail (100-km) in under 48 hours.

Highlights from 2015 included:

  • Assembling 2 teams of 4 hikers each with no prior hiking experience
  • As team captain, planning and leading over 30+ training sessions under various weather conditions through various hiking trails
  • Arranging coaching by Trailwalker veterans such as Mr. KK Chan of Cosmoboys and Trailwalker Teaching Room
  • Organizing various fundraising activities and raising over HK$240,000.

You can read more about our efforts in articles featured in the Chamber Bulletin!

  • Hiking for a Good Cause (July 2015)
  • Gearing Up for Trailwalker (October 2015)
  • From Agony to Jubilation (December 2015)
  • Donations (February 2016)
